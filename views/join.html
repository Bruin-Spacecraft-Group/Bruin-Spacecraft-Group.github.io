Join the Team

Learn how you can get involved

Scientist or artist, policy-maker or engineer, our only requirement is a passion for space.

Find your Place

There are many opportunities at Bruin Space, ranging from science and engineering to finance, art, and public relations.

  • For those interested in spacecraft engineering, consider joining Overseer, Reach, or Sandbox.
  • To explore space science, check out Endeavour.
  • If you want to develop industry contacts, handle corporate relations, and public outreach efforts, consider our External Affairs team.
  • For experience managing accounts and applying for funds, join the Finance team.
  • If you are looking for a way to express your passion for space through art or share your excitement with the world, check out our Marketing team.

How to Join

Here are a few things you need to do to get started:

  • Join our slack team, and consider downloading the app. Slack's a pretty big deal here!
  • Join our mailing list, for biweekly updates and opportunities.
  • Complete lab safety training through UCLA's EH&S department.
  • Attend a meeting!
    • General meetings are a perfect introduction to the organization as a whole, but we encourage you to attend a project meeting and get a firsthand look at what it is we do.
    • For a schedule of all Bruin Space meetings, check out our calendar.

Slack

Slack is a communication platform built for teams. Channels allow information to be exchanged with those it impacts the most. You’ll automatically be added to #bruinspace, where all official, group-wide communication happens, and #deorbit, where we share space news, organize social events and stuff! Our slack domain, bruinspace.slack.com is accessible to anyone with a UCLA email, but if you don’t have a UCLA email, just message us on facebook or shoot us an email and we’ll get you set up.
